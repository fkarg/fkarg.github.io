# Review

## Main Takeaways
- give people as much agency as you can
- trust and delegation over control
- reduce dependency on single 'controllers'
- increase performance by enabling actual ownership
- leader-leader over follower-follower
- actually listen


- Mechanisms for CONTROL, 

- 'very well'
- conscious action
- reduced buerocracy
- don't brief, certify
- 'Chiefs in Charge'
- communicate early, regularly



### Core of Leader-Leader
is GIVING employees CONTROL, with the enabling pillars of COMPETENCE and CLARITY.

### Control
- find the genetic code (who is responsible for what) and rewrite it
- act your way to new thinking (?)
- short, early conversations for efficient work
- use 'I intend to...' to turn passive followers to active leaders
- resist the urge to provide top-down solutions
- eliminate top-down monitoring/controlling systems
- think out loud (both superiours and subordinates)
- embrace the inspectors, actively learn from them

### Competence
- take deliberate action
- we learn (everywhere, everytime)
- don't brief, certify
- continually and consistently repeat the message
    - people might interpret different things. repeat it, they will figure it out eventually
    - specifically the goals and how you wnat them to operate
- specify goals, not methods

### Clarity
- achieve excellence, don't just avoid errors
- build trust and take of your people
- use your legacy for inspiration
- immediately (and publicly) recognize desired behaviour
- begin with the end in mind
- encourage questioning attitude over blind obedience
