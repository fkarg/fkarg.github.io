Checklist to consider before publishing a blog post.

## Making it easy to read
- [ ] Use short sentences. If it's too hard to parse, people won't.
- [ ] Is grammar and spelling good? Check with aspell/LanguageTool
- [ ] Are there sufficient examples for new concepts?
- [ ] Pay attention to motivation: Why should they read it, what _value_ does it give _them_?
- [ ] Focus your energy on the beginnings. This makes skimming a lot easier
    - [ ] Each Paragraph
    - [ ] The Post as a whole
- [ ] Use lots of short paragraphs. A paragraph should correspond to a single idea.
- [ ] Anticipate difficult arguments and proactively assuage them. Give positive and especially negative examples.
- [ ] Which mistakes did you make when learning about it? Steer readers away from common mistakes and misconceptions
- [ ] End on a high note. Leave people with a good feeling. (e.g. Inspirational call to action)
- [ ] Tell them what you're going to tell them. Then tell them. Finally, tell them what you told them. - Unknown

## Publishing
- [ ] ran `aspell check \<file\>`
- [ ] added date and moved file to `_post`
- [ ] change `published` to `true`
