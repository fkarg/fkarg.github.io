## Participation in Startup competition
Well, this something that is in progress currently, and the first lessens have
been learned already. There will be a more detailed post about them in the
future. The basic reason for participation was that I wanted to know more about
what realizing a certain idea would take. One of the initial takeaways: Ideas
are cheap, it's all about execution. I have a lot of ideas and talk about them
with a lot of people, but it's hard to evaluate if it is worth to continue
thinking about it further or not. So in going all the way once, I hope to be
able to more accurately estimate what is required to test the idea at hand.
