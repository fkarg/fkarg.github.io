something like that: It is _impossible_ to have a 'neutral' information

It's hard to stay neutral about something that is not even important. So how
should it be possible for anyone to stay neutral as soon as it has value?
- not possible for professor to stay neutral about other lectures of his
- how should it be possible for media outlets to stay neutral when money (and
  clicks) are involved?
- what does that say about us, about reality, and about the media portraying it?


Implication: staying up-to-date with the news is guaranteed to give you
a massively distorted picture of reality.
