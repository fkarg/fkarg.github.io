So I had this female friend whom I had some feelings for. First red flag. Because she did not.

I projected onto her a lot that she was not (Red Flags):
- kind, understanding, my ally
- I wanted her to be someone I could show my love in full force

Sex
- It was not so much about the sex itself as it was validation received from
  it; that I was someone loveable
- I was insecure about my performance, and it showed (though in the end I was
  not even that bad overall)

- I blew it by
    - wanting to continuously please her, to the degree of somewhat resenting
      her (~>not holding frame)
    - depending on her for validation
    - not doing my own thing and 'following' her instead
    - not being the captain of the 'relationship'
    - initiating the relationship talk first
    - being insecure about 'finishing too early'
    - breaking down mentally in front of her for mental support
    - not being able to hold my frame for shit-tests sufficiently
    - my ego requiring sex as validation for success
        (which is kind of true, but not like that)
