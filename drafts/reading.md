# Reading


## Reasons for Reading
There are many reasons for reading something. They include entertainment,
curiosity, or research. Reading for entertainent could be a good novel, such as
any from asimov, or the works of shakespheare. Being curious, wanting to aquire
knowledge is another good reason to read a book. But is it?

## Akquiring Knowledge
There are roughly three different categories of knowledge.

- Facts
- Practices
- Concepts

Facts include things like when the first dishwasher was invented, when IBM was
founded, or what the dimensions of your bed are.

Practices include everything from how to repair a bike, to eating and what to
watch out for when buying a new laptop.

Concepts are the most abstract, since they might be something that might not
even exist. A concept, such as that of a lion, implies more than just the facts
related to to it. It also implies the danger of being around it. The danger is
considerably lessened when you are in a zoo

or a really good friendship, if
you grew up with it.
