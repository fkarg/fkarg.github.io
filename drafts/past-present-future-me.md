# Past, Present, and Future Me

Options on how to deal with this relationship, and positive/negative parts:

expectation on past me, acted to/with:
* the best of knowledge
    + will not be blamed when better options are available in hindsight
    - will be blamed, if knew better option but did not act upon it (procrastination/akrasia)
* the best of intention
    + will not be blamed for bad results, when intentions where pure
    - hard to determine pure/unpure intentions (especially for self, due to ego)
    - will be blamed for having had bad or misled intentions (which is human)
* the best of ability
    + will not be blamed, when solution was out of reach
    - will be blamed, when solution was not attempted
* improve the situation (Value any progress)
    + Showing appreciation and gratitude for work done
    + prevents buildup of negative feelings
    - ignores obvious potential for improvement (might be fine to look at these seperately?)
* was not actively working against the situation or missed known/available/easy
  chances or deadlines of own fault
    - 
* no selfish intentions (only benefiting in the present)
    - might consider breaks as selfish, and blame past me (might result in high tendency for burnout)
    + will result in effective work
    + might be nuanced, but might be best option available
