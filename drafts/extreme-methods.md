Everyone _can_ get to the top. But to get there, you need to put in the effort. _extreme_ effort. 

Make people sick and angry with how dedicated you are.

But if you don't have something you are extreme about - and you are not putting
in extreme "what the fuck is wrong with that guy" levels of dedication, then
you will not have the escape velocity you need to find deliverance from the
"decent" zone.
