How come, that while the internet is full of pictures and videos of cats, most
of what I deliberately consume is pure text? And it's not just me, either. Both
[LessWrong][lw] and [HackerNews][hn] are examples of full-fledged subcultures
like that. Even parts of [Reddit][reddit] are like that. Why? Here's some
thoughts.


* TOC
{:toc}
---

- We value text: while most of the internet prefers pictures and videos for
  entertainment, we prefer to have information as text. Why? because while
  pictures/videos are certainly helpful, they rarely can describe the full
  picture with the necessary nuance.
