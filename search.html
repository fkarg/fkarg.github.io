When searching, note the following:
  • Every post with at least one word matching is shown
  • Posts are sorted based on an overall 'match score'
  • Occurences in url, title or subtitle are weighed more
  • Occurences of multiple search terms are weighed more
  • Fuzzy matching (up to edit distance one) is active
  • Matching the beginning of word is a full match
