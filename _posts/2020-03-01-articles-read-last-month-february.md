PowerPoint Makes Us Stupid. Here Are 3 Smarter Alternatives | Inc.com
https://www.inc.com/geoffrey-james/sick-of-powerpoint-heres-what-to-use-instead.html

Key takeaways:
1. If you need to discuss and decide, use a briefing document.
2. If you must instruct or train, create interactive experiences.
3. If you want to entertain or inspire, give a speech.

> That's the point that Steve Jobs was making when he famously said: "People
> who know what they are talking about don't need PowerPoint." If you truly
> know what you're talking about, you won't take the lazy way out (for you) and
> simply do a presentation. Do the job right and your audience will love you
> for it.
