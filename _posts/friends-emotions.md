The basic idea is that people in and by themselves are not your friends, only
the emotional state they are in is. Because they certainly also have other
emotional states, in which you are not their friend.

And this is fine, you are the same too. 

It's close to impossible to be friendly with someone while they are being
agitated about something. _They_ are not your friend, their emotions are.
