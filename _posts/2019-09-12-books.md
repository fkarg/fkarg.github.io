### The Process
I own a lot of books. In fact, so many that I should get rid of some of them.
And yet I still buy new books regularly. Or get one as a present. And I still
like it. And I still want to get more.

Every day after waking up, I first write for about 30min. About whatever comes
to mind, really. Afterwards, I read. The goal is to read for 30min, but that's
rarely the case. Usually it's about 60min, sometimes up to 90min.

While reading, I take notes. I published these notes in my first book reviews
as well. I don't do that anymore, but I do give a short summary, and most of
the review is what I think about the book, and my favorite quotes from it.

This is just an overview, I have some more (unlisted) books, some of which I do
not intend to write about. Which book I choose or get next is due to my
arbitration, though you might be able to influence that by bying a specific
book or writing a comment :)
