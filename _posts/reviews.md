This describes a process by which we've been doing a monthly review for over a
year now (with modifications).

General Schedule:
15min Socializing
30min Review für jeden selbst
5min Pause
20min Review erzählen
20min Planning
5min Pause
20min Review erzählen
20min Planning
5min Schedule next meeting & Closing
\_\_\_\_\_\_
2h15min mit 2 x 5min Pause
kleinerer Review-call (~30min) alle 4 wochen um 2 wochen versetzt


Current adaptions:
usually reduced reviewing time (20min)
Combined silent planning time (10-20min), and more of 'presenting' the plan
    than interactively thinking through it

frequent questions as to 'why is it you want to do this' or 'what about trying X, would that work for you'
